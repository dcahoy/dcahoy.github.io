Dexter Cahoy

 

 

I am a Professor of Statistics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at University of Houston - Downtown (UHD). Before joining UHD, I was a faculty member in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Louisiana Tech University for 10 years. I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from Case Western Reserve University in 2007. I play tennis or basketball for fun. I am originally from Cagayan de Oro City, Philippines.
